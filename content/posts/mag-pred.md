## Central Question
Could you possibly build a compuationally light earthquake predicting algorithm? 

### Short answer
No. Earthquake prediction, especially time predictions is pretty much impossible. However, using aftershock mechanics and regional seismic activity, one could build an algorithm that predicts the maximum magnitude an earthquake could have.
